1. What is Arbitrage?

Arbitrage is the process of simultaneous buying and selling of an asset from different platforms, exchanges or locations to cash in on the price difference (usually small in percentage terms)?

3. Is arbitrage 100% risk free?

Arbitrage refers to the practice of simultaneously buying and selling an investment in order to profit from a difference in price. Essentially, arbitrage can exist because of inefficiencies in the market, and if an arbitrage is found, it can be a risk-free way to earn a profit.

Although this may seem like a complicated transaction to the untrained eye, arbitrage trades are actually quite straightforward and are thus considered low-risk.
